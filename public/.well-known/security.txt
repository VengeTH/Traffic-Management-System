# Security Policy
We take security seriously. If you discover a security vulnerability, please follow responsible disclosure:

1. Do not disclose the vulnerability publicly until we have addressed it
2. Provide detailed information about the vulnerability
3. Allow us reasonable time to fix the issue before disclosure
4. Act in good faith and avoid accessing or modifying data that does not belong to you

# Scope
This security policy applies to:
- The E-VioPay application and API
- All subdomains of eviopay.laspinas.gov.ph

# Out of Scope
- Social engineering attacks
- Physical attacks
- Denial of service attacks
- Issues requiring physical access to devices

# Recognition
We appreciate security researchers who help us keep our systems secure. With your permission, we may acknowledge your contribution.
